# Domain 3 Survey Questions (Temporal / Seasonal Factors)

Time window: LAST 14 DAYS

Goal:
Capture short-term environmental events and disruptions that can temporarily increase diarrhoea risk.

## Q1. Recent events (multi-select)
In the past 14 days, has your community experienced any of the following: a heatwave/very hot weather, heavy rainfall, or flooding? You can say more than one, or say "none".

Allowed concepts:
- Heatwave / very hot weather
- Heavy rainfall / downpour / monsoon-like rain
- Flooding / floodwaters
- None

## Q2. Most recent event timing (date OR days ago)
About when did the MOST RECENT event happen? Please give a date (YYYY-MM-DD) or how many days ago (0–14). If none, say "none".

## Q2a. (Only if Q2 unclear) Days ago (0–14)
If you're not sure of the exact date, about how many days ago was it (0–14)?

## Q3. Community diarrhoea outbreak signal (Yes/No)
In the past 14 days, have you heard of many people in your community having diarrhoea? Please answer Yes or No.

## Q4. Water supply interruption (Yes/No)
In the past 14 days, has your household had any water supply interruptions? Please answer Yes or No.

## Q4a. (Only if Q4=Yes) Longer water storage (Yes/No)
Did the interruption cause you to store water longer than usual? Please answer Yes or No.
